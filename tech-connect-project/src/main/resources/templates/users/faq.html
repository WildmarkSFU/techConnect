We're Here To Help

What is TechConnect?

TechConnect is a platform that connects users with the owner to help solve their tech-related problems.

How do I use TechConnect?

Filing an inquiry will then create a chat with the owner. You can then have real time conversations with him.

How long do I have to wait for an answer?

The owner will be notified any time you send a message. So whenever he sees it, he can reply back to you!

Why TechConnect?

Quickly send and receive messages. By providing the urgency of your question, your question can be answered in minutes.

How do I create an inquiry?

To create an inquiry, log in to your account and click on the "New Inquiry" button on your dashboard. Fill out the inquiry form and submit it to get help from the expert himself!
